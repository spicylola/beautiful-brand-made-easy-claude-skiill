05 — Voice & Tone

Do

✓  Write in sentence case — never ALL CAPS in running copy
✓  Lead with the seller benefit — "90% goes to you. Always."
✓  Use "skills" not "prompts" or "templates"
✓  Keep copy short and direct
✓  Be warm and inclusive — for everyone, not just developers
✓  Use green sparingly — it should pop when it appears

Don't

✗  Don't use more than one accent color
✗  Don't use Prata below 12px or in bold
✗  Don't add gradients, shadows, or decorative effects
✗  Don't make it feel like a developer tool
✗  Don't introduce new colors without design review
✗  Don't use green as a large area background fill
