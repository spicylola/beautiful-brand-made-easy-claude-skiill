## What You'll Build

Up to **4 HTML files** — your complete brand system:

**Brand Kit** (`[brand]-brand-kit.html`)
Your foundation. Full color palette with hex codes, typography system with font specimens, wordmark in 3 treatments, UI components, and a voice & tone guide.

**Website Mockup** (`[brand]-website-mockup.html`)
A full responsive page mockup — landing, about, pricing, services, or waitlist — with nav, hero, features, testimonials, CTA, and footer. All in your exact brand.

**Physical Mockups** (`[brand]-physical-mockups.html`)
See your brand in the real world. CSS/SVG illustrations of your brand on a mug, tote bag, poster, product label, phone case, and t-shirt.

**Business Card** (`[brand]-business-card.html`)
Print-ready front + back at 3.5" × 2" with bleed guides. Export to PDF via browser print.

All files are fully self-contained HTML — shareable, printable, and ready to hand to a developer, printer, or client.

## How The Conversation Works

Claude guides you one question at a time — never rushing, never overwhelming. Here's the flow:

1. Brand name, description, audience, personality words, tagline
2. **Moodboard & vibe** — share Pinterest boards, photos, Instagram accounts, anything that captures the feeling
3. **Color inspiration** — paste a URL to scan, share a palette screenshot, or let Claude build options for you
4. **Font inspiration** — share a reference or let Claude show you curated pairings to choose from
5. **Logo inspiration** — share references or Claude creates a clean text wordmark
6. Claude writes a creative brief and waits for your approval
7. Brand kit is built — then you choose what to create next

At every step, Claude checks in before moving on. You go at your own pace.
