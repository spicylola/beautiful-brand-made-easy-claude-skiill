05 — Voice & Tone
Do
✓
Speak with quiet authority — confident, not loud. The brand knows what it's doing.
✓
Be precise. Use data to back everything up. Numbers build trust with this audience.
✓
Inspire without preaching. "You deserve this" — not "you need to do this."
✓
Acknowledge the struggle. Corporate burnout is real. Meet them where they are.
✓
Keep copy lean. Every word earns its place. Silence is also a design choice.
✓
Use the tagline like a mantra — repeat it until they believe it.
Don't
✗
Don't shout. No excessive exclamation marks. FitAF doesn't need to beg for attention.
✗
Don't shame. This is about aspiration, not guilt. Never use language that punishes.
✗
Don't use bro-culture fitness clichés. "No pain no gain" is not FitAF.
✗
Don't over-explain. Trust the user's intelligence. They're corporate — they're smart.
✗
Don't use generic wellness language like "holistic journey" or "wellness ecosystem."
✗
Don't be cold or clinical. Precision with warmth — not a doctor's report.
